Delicious Beef Burger

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Hamburuger bun
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to 450 degrees F
  2. Prepare lettuce by chopping into bun sized sections
  3. Mix the groun beef with salt and pepper and form into a patty
  4. Place the burugers on the grill and cook for 8 minutes, flipping halfway through
  5. Place a slicec of cheddar cheese on the patty for 1 minute to melt
  6. Place the patty on the bun and add desired toppings
  7. Enjoy!
