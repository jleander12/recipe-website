Best Homemade Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Roll the pizza dough into a circle with constant thickness of 1/8 - 1/4 inch
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly in the center of the pizza
  4. Place a layer of mozzarella cheese on top of the tomato sauce
  5. Put the pizza in the oven for 8 minutes
  6. Pull the pizza out of the oven and sprinkle basil
  7. Serve hot!
