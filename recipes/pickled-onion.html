Quick Pickled Onions

Ingredients

  • Red onion
  • White vinegar
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Chop red onion into thin pieces and stuff into a jar
  2. Mix white vinegar and honey and pour into a sauce pan
  3. Heat the mixture until it begins simmering
  4. Pour the vinegar mixture into the jar of onions
  5. Cover and let cool for 30 minutes
  6. They are ready to be served!
